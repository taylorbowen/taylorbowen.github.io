Has Design become too hard?

Article by Jeffery Zeldman

"Designers love to complain. It’s our nature. After all, if we were satisfied with the way things work, feel, and look, we wouldn’t have become designers in the first place."

Not only learning the rules, but being able to not follow them and still have a solid piece of work. Being able to use the rules and break them.
